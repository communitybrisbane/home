About Community_Brisbane

make your days count.

Community_Brisbane is a community that hosts international exchange events around Brisbane CBD.

We aim to make life in Brisbane fun, create opportunities to make new friends, and lower the barriers to overseas challenges.

We provide a place where everyone can easily interact.

EVENTS

Main Activities

International Exchange

We regularly host events where people of various nationalities living in Brisbane can interact. It's a great opportunity to find new connections beyond language and cultural barriers. We prepare various activities so that everyone can enjoy.

Japanese Culture Exchange

We host events where you can experience Japanese culture and spread the charm of Japan. We plan events on various themes such as calligraphy, anime, cooking, and summer festivals. We aim to spread Japanese culture in Brisbane.

Working Holiday Support

We provide various support such as life information and job hunting advice for those who come to Brisbane on working holidays. We provide a place for local information exchange and consultation, and support you to live overseas life with peace of mind.

I started this community because I wanted to make many friends overseas and enjoy life to the fullest.
